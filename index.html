How to Play

 
  • Player ROLLs dice
    •  
  • Player SELECTs dice based on dice combo sheet to score points.
    •  
  • Player may choose to ROLL again with the remaining dice that has not yet been selected.
    •  
  • If a player ROLLs and no points are SELECTed on ROLL, all points that turn are lost.
    •  
  • A player may choose to HOLD instead, ending their turn while locking in the points earned.
    •  
  • If all dice are SELECTed for points during a player's turn, all dice will be reset and that player continue their turn.
    •  
  • First player to hit 10,000 points wins!!!
    •  
 

Dice Combos

 
  • 6-of-a-kind     = 3,000 pts
  • Two triplets    = 2,500 pts
  • 5-of-a-kind     = 2,000 pts
  • Straight (1-6)  = 1,500 pts
  • Three pairs     = 1,500 pts
  • Four and a Pair = 1,500 pts
  • 4-of-a-kind     = 1,000 pts
  • 6, 6, 6       = 600 pts
  • 5, 5, 5       = 500 pts
  • 4, 4, 4       = 400 pts
  • 3, 3, 3       = 300 pts
  • 2, 2, 2       = 200 pts
  • 1, 1, 1       = 300 pts
  • Ones          = 100 pts
  • Fives        = 50 pts
